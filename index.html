How We Work

A deliberate process.
Every engagement.

From first conversation to ongoing optimization — a structured approach that eliminates guesswork and keeps every party accountable.

01

Discovery & Audit

Before recommending a single tactic, we conduct a thorough audit of your current digital presence, competitive landscape, target audience, and business objectives. This forms the factual foundation of everything that follows.

✓ Delivered as a written audit report
02

Strategy Development

A channel-specific marketing strategy is documented and presented for your review and approval. This strategy defines target personas, key messages, channel mix, KPIs, and a 90-day execution roadmap. Nothing proceeds without your sign-off.

✓ Delivered as a strategy document for your records
03

Retainer Agreement

We formalize the engagement with a clear monthly retainer agreement that outlines exact deliverables, revision rights, communication expectations, response time SLAs, and cancellation terms. You will always know precisely what you are paying for.

✓ Signed agreement provided before any work begins
04

Creative Execution & Delivery

Campaigns are built and submitted for your approval before going live. All creative assets, copy, and campaign structures are shared in advance. We operate on a content calendar so you always know what is coming and when.

✓ Approval required before any content is published
05

Monthly Reporting & Strategy Review

At the end of each month, you receive a comprehensive performance report and join a strategy call to review results, discuss what the data is telling us, and agree on priorities for the following month. No surprises, no spin.

✓ Written report delivered before each monthly call
06

Optimize & Scale

Data from every campaign informs the next. We continuously refine targeting, messaging, and channel allocation to improve performance over time. When something is working, we invest more in it. When it is not, we adapt quickly.

✓ Ongoing optimization included in all retainer plans

Common Questions

What you need to know.

What does the retainer agreement include?

Every plan includes a signed agreement that defines exact monthly deliverables, revision rights, response time SLAs, approval workflows, and cancellation terms. You will never wonder what you are paying for.

How do I cancel?

Any plan can be cancelled with 30 days' written notice. There are no annual commitments, no early termination fees, and no penalty clauses. We earn your business every single month.

What happens in the first month?

The first month begins with our discovery and audit process, followed by strategy development and your approval. Execution begins once the strategy is signed off. We do not rush to publish before the foundation is right.

Do ad spend costs come out of the retainer?

No. The retainer covers our management and creative fees. Advertising spend (Google, Meta, etc.) is billed separately and goes directly to the platforms. We will recommend a budget during the strategy phase.

Can I upgrade or downgrade plans?

Yes. You can move between plans with 30 days' notice. If your business needs change, we will adjust the engagement accordingly with a revised retainer agreement.

Who will I be working with?

You will work directly with our founding team throughout the engagement. We do not delegate client relationships to junior staff. Your calls, messages, and approvals all go to the people doing the work.
