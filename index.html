How We Work

A deliberate process. Every engagement.

From first conversation to ongoing optimization — a structured approach that eliminates guesswork and keeps every party accountable.

01

Discovery & Audit

Before recommending a single tactic, we conduct a thorough audit of your current digital presence, competitive landscape, target audience, and business objectives. This forms the factual foundation of everything that follows.

Delivered as a written audit report
02

Strategy Development

A channel-specific marketing strategy is documented and presented for your review and approval. This defines target personas, key messages, channel mix, KPIs, and a 90-day execution roadmap. Nothing proceeds without your sign-off.

Delivered as a strategy document for your records
03

Retainer Agreement

We formalize the engagement with a clear monthly retainer agreement that outlines exact deliverables, revision rights, communication expectations, response time SLAs, and cancellation terms. You will always know precisely what you are paying for.

Signed agreement provided before any work begins

Campaigns are built and submitted for your approval before going live. All creative assets, copy, and campaign structures are shared in advance. We operate on a content calendar so you always know what is coming and when.

Written report delivered before each monthly call

Optimize & Scale
